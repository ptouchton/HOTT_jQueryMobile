Led Zeppelin Studio Albums

  • Led Zeppelin

    Debut album. Songs include "Good Times Bad Times", "Dazed and Confused" and "Communication Breakdown".

  • Led Zeppelin II

    Second studio album. Songs include "Whole Lotta Love", "Thanks You", "Heartbreaker" and "Ramble On".

  • Led Zeppelin III

    Third studio album. Songs include "Immigrant Song", "Since I've Been Loving You" and "Tangerine".

  • Led Zeppelin IV

    Untitled fourth album (commonly referred to as Led Zeppelin IV).  Songs include "Black Dog", "Rock and Roll" and "Stairway to Heaven".

  • Houses of the Holy

    Fifth studio album.  Songs include "The Song Remains the Same", "D'yer Maker" and "Dancing Days".

  • Physical Graffiti

    Sixth studio album.  Songs include "Houses of the Holy", "Trampled Under Foot" and "Kashmir".

  • Presence

    Seventh studio album.  Songs include "Nobody's Fault but Mine" and "Candy Store Rock".

  • In Through the Out Door

    Eighth studio album.  Songs include "In the Evening", "Fool in the Rain" and "All My Love".

  • Coda

    Ninth and final studio album. Songs include "I Can't Quit You Baby" and "Bonzo's Montreux".
