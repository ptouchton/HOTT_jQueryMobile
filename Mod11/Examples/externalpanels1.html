External Panels - Page 1

The two panels are defined outside the page.  Panels are typically defined as siblings to the header, footer and content containers within the page.  

Panels outside of a page must be initialized manually and will not be handled by auto init.  Panels outside of pages will remain in the DOM (unless manually removed) as long as you use Ajax navigation, and can be opened or closed from any page.
