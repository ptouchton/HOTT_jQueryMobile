Panel with Fixed Position

Menu Add

The left button will display a panel containing content that is larger than the page.  The framework will check the height of the panel contents and "unfix" the panel so its content is scrollable.


























The page content is longer in order to demonstrate the fixed positioning of the panel.  Both the header and footer of the page are also fixed.
